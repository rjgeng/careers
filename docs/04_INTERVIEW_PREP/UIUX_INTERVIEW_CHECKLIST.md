# UI/UX Interview Preparation Checklist

## 1. Product Thinking
- Can explain constraints and tradeoffs
- Can define success metrics for UX outcomes
- Can articulate problem framing, not just solutions

## 2. Interaction Design
- Clear, structured flows
- Error states and edge cases considered
- Accessibility incorporated early

## 3. Visual & Information Design
- Hierarchy and layout choices explained
- Consistency with design systems
- Responsiveness considerations

## 4. Collaboration & Communication
- Works effectively with PM and engineering
- Can give and receive critique constructively
- Communicates design reasoning clearly
